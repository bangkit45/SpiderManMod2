Spider-Man No Way Home Addon many versions of Spider-Man, his enemies and other characters in Minecraft Bedrock. You get new mobs and craft masks to become Spider-Man, Venom and various other types of Spider-Man. A great addition for fans of this comic.









Installation:
— Download McPack
— Use the Minecraft
— Install McAddon or McPack files, just open it for this;
— Select new textures in the settings;
— Done.
